## 1. Agreement Context

IF:
- The company is acting as **Service Provider**, AND
- The **standard MSA template** is used, AND
- The agreement is a **renewal with no material changes**

THEN:
- **Auto-approve**
- Proceed to execution

ELSE:
- Route to **Legal review**

---

## 2. Commercial Information

IF:
- Total Contract Value (TCV) is **within approved financial thresholds**, AND
- Contract term is **within standard duration**, AND
- No revenue, usage, or volume-based commitments exceed approved limits

THEN:
- Continue under **standard approval workflow**

ELSE:
- Route to **Legal and Finance review**

---

## 3. Limitation of Liability and Indemnity

IF:
- Changes requested are **within approved thresholds**, AND
- Liability is capped at **fees paid in the preceding 12 months**, AND
- Indemnity obligations are **mutual**, AND
- No new or expanded **carve-outs** are introduced, AND
- No indemnities are **uncapped or excluded** from the liability cap

THEN:
- Continue under **standard approval workflow**

ELSE:
- Route to **Legal review**
- Route to **Finance review** if financial exposure exceeds approved thresholds

---

## 4. Intellectual Property

IF:
- No deviations from **standard IP ownership provisions**, AND
- Licence scope remains **limited and non-transferable** (where the company is Service Provider)

THEN:
- Continue under **standard approval workflow**

ELSE:
- Route to **Legal and Finance review**

---

## 5. Data and Confidentiality

IF:
- No **personal data** is processed under the agreement, AND
- No execution of a **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** is required

THEN:
- Continue under **standard approval workflow**

ELSE:
- Route to **Legal review**
- Trigger **DPA workflow**, where applicable

---

## 6. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

IF:
- Governing law remains **unchanged**, AND
- Dispute resolution mechanism aligns with **approved company policy**

THEN:
- Continue under **standard approval workflow**

ELSE:
- Route to **Legal review**

---

## 7. Urgency and Business Priority

IF:
- Requested execution timeline is **within pre-approved thresholds**, AND
- Urgency is **business-justified**

THEN:
- **Expedite review** under applicable workflow

ELSE:
- Process under **standard timelines**
