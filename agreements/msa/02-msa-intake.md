## Purpose
To capture the minimum required information to assess legal and financial risk, determine routing logic, and apply automation rules for Master Services Agreements (MSAs).

---

## Intake Questions

### 1. Agreement Context
a. Is the company acting as a **Service Provider** or **Customer**?  
b. Is the **standard MSA template** being used?  
c. Is this a **new agreement** or a **renewal**?

---

### 2. Commercial Information
a. What is the **Total Contract Value (TCV)**?  
b. What are the **contract terms** (initial term and renewal terms)?  
c. Are there any **revenue, usage, or volume-based commitments**?

---

### 3. Limitation of Liability and Indemnity
a. Are any **changes requested** to the limitation of liability clause?  
b. Is liability capped at **fees paid in the preceding 12 months**?  
c. Are **indemnity obligations mutual**?  
d. Are any indemnities **uncapped** or **excluded from the liability cap**?

---

### 4. Intellectual Property
a. Are there any **deviations from standard IP ownership provisions**?  
b. Does the **licence scope** include sublicensing or commercial exploitation?

---

### 5. Data and Confidentiality
a. Will any **personal data** be processed under this agreement?  
b. Does the agreement require execution of a **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)**?

---

### 6. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution
a. What is the **governing law**?  
b. What are the **dispute resolution mechanisms** (courts / arbitration)?

---

### 7. Urgency and Business Priority
a. What is the **requested execution timeline**?  
b. What is the **business justification for urgency**, if any?
